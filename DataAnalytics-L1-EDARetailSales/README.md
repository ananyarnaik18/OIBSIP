# Task 1 — EDA on Retail Sales Data

## Track
Data Analytics (Level 1)

## Objective
Perform a thorough Exploratory Data Analysis on a retail sales dataset to uncover patterns, customer behaviour trends, and actionable business insights.

## Dataset
Superstore Sales Dataset (train.csv) — sourced from Kaggle.
Contains 9,800 rows and 18 columns including Order Date, Ship Date, Customer, Segment, Region, Category, Product, and Sales.

## Tech Stack
Python, pandas, matplotlib, seaborn, Jupyter Notebook

## Key Steps Performed
- Data inspection: shape, dtypes, null value check
- Descriptive statistics (mean, median, mode, std) for numerical columns
- Time series analysis: monthly and quarterly sales trends
- Customer segment breakdown (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office)
- Product analysis: top 10 best-selling products and revenue by category
- Correlation heatmap on engineered numeric features
- Additional visualization: sales by day of week
- Business recommendations based on findings

## Key Insights
- Sales distribution is highly right-skewed (mean ₹230.77 vs median ₹54.49)
- Clear year-end seasonality with an overall upward trend (2015–2019)
- Consumer segment drives 52.1% of the customer base
- A single product (Canon imageCLASS 2200 Advanced Copier) significantly outperforms all others in revenue
